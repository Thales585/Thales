## Backtest as evidence

Survivor +25.4% / −9.3%. The naive EUAD/DAL pair beat it (+41.3% / −5.3%) — but that
pair is an engine plus its own hedge with no toll collection, and DAL's contribution is
a rear-view rally. The RTX-engine alternative (+34.6%) was declined on K2 grounds argued
above: the book buys the thesis, not the neighborhood. Both stand as recorded evidence
against the construction if the mechanism fails.
