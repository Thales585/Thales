# Housing gauntlet — pre-registration (written before data pull)

- **Run opened:** 2026-07-04 · Horizon 12m Jul 2026→Jul 2027 · USD · buy-and-hold
- **Pool:** Engine DHI, LEN (volume builders). Tollbooth BLDR (primary), FNF and HD
  (widening: title fees per transaction; repair-and-remodel flow). Inverse INVH, AMH
  (single-family rental REITs).

## The BLDR question, decided in advance

BLDR qualifies as tollbooth only under the flow-collector reading: it supplies every
builder regardless of who wins share. It **dies at K2** if its economics are
demonstrably commodity pass-through (lumber P&L) rather than value-added volume margin.
FNF is the purer per-transaction fee collector and takes the seat if BLDR fires K2 and
FNF survives its own kills.

## Kill criteria (execution only, no mid-run amendment)

K1 cap ≥ $500M, no going concern. K2 seat authenticity per above; INVH/AMH die if
rent growth is not actually resilient in the high-rate regime (the seat's whole claim).
K3 positive trailing-12m TR and maxDD > −60%. K4 no forced dilutive raise in window.
K5 pairwise monthly corr < 0.9 vs occupied seat — expected tangle: INVH vs AMH (same
business; at most one takes the seat), DHI vs LEN (engine contention, loser discarded).

## Scenario claim + uninsured quadrant (named now)

High-rates-for-longer starves builders, feeds SFR rents from the priced-out. Rates-cut
boom: builders and BLDR feed, SFR gets cap-rate relief — non-cannibalization holds.
**Uninsured quadrant: rates high AND rents soft** (sunbelt supply glut + frozen
resale market) — all three starve.

## Weights default

Engine 40–45 / Tollbooth 30–35 / Inverse 25–30; enact $5,000 paper if a trio survives.
