# The Hypothesis Register — reverse-engineered from the book

*Compiled 2026-07-05. Every position in the book is a bet on a mechanism. This register
extracts each mechanism as a falsifiable hypothesis (H#), states what evidence would
confirm or reject it, and cross-references the exit triggers in
[reports/2026-07-05-exit-triggers.md](../reports/2026-07-05-exit-triggers.md). At the
horizon, each H gets a verdict: confirmed / rejected / unresolved-by-noise.*

## Playbook trios (inherited, enacted 2026-07-04)

**H1 — Uranium (NXE/CCJ/EQT):** *A structural uranium supply deficit will reassert
pricing power for developers and fuel-cycle incumbents within 12 months, independent of
the equity drawdown that preceded entry.* Confirmed by: term-price strength + NXE/CCJ
outperformance around dated catalysts (contract signings, earnings). Rejected by:
term price rolling over while equities stagnate, or the "thesis-external decline"
proving thesis-internal (demand destruction).

**H2 — Electrons (LIT*/NGG/FLNC):** *Electrification demand is a durable regime on the
US–China policy axis; grid transmission collects regardless of which battery chemistry
or maker wins.* (*LIT is a diluted CATL proxy — H2's engine grade must cite CATL
fundamentals, not LIT price.) Confirmed by: CATL volume/margin execution + NGG
regulated-return stability. Rejected by: policy-axis rupture (tariff walls collapsing
battery trade), or FLNC failing as storage inverse.

**H3 — Metals (GLD/WPM/PPLT):** *The monetary-debasement bid persists; streaming
economics outcollect miners; platinum is the recovery-regime hedge.* Confirmed by: WPM
margin capture at locked costs while gold holds. Rejected by: real-rate regime shift
that breaks gold with no industrial handoff to platinum.

**H4 — Procurement (XMTR/FAST/RBA):** *Industrial procurement digitization grows
through the cycle; distribution tolls all activity; auction volumes feed on distress.*
Pre-registered caveat: engine entered richly valued. Rejected by: XMTR growth stalling
at rich multiple (timing rejection, logged as such), FAST margin erosion.

## Gauntlet-born trios (2026-07-04/05)

**H5 — Agriculture (CTVA/ADM/TSN):** *Farmer economics and processor volumes diverge
under a grain-glut regime: seed pricing power erodes, crush volume persists, protein
margins expand on cheap feed.* Confirmed by: TSN margin expansion coinciding with soft
grain + CTVA pricing pressure (the seesaw working). Rejected by: all three moving on
one macro axis with no differential (mechanism absent), or ADM's toll proving to be
trading P&L (K2 latent failure).

**H6 — Housing (DHI/ESNT/INVH):** *In a frozen-transaction, high-rate regime, tolls on
flow starve but tolls on the in-force stock keep collecting; rents capture the
priced-out.* This is the run's discovered hypothesis — the gauntlet found it by killing
the flow-toll pool. Confirmed by: ESNT premium yield stability + INVH rent growth while
builder margins compress. Rejected by: a foreclosure wave converting the MI toll into a
claims-payer (the wart realizing), or a rate-cut thaw (regime change, graded as P&L
event, not rejection).

**H7 — GLP-1 (LLY/MCK/DVA):** *Obesity-franchise economics accrue to the leader; the
distribution toll is molecule-agnostic; chronic-kidney caseloads persist if renal
promises disappoint.* Warts at entry: engine rich, inverse pre-paid (+67% trailing).
Confirmed by: LLY obesity revenue compounding + MCK fee stability. Rejected by:
chain-wide pricing legislation (the named uninsured quadrant firing — that is a
*regime* rejection all the same, because the quadrant was knowingly uninsured).

**H8 — Defense (EUAD/HEI/DAL):** *European rearmament is a multi-year budget regime,
not a headline trade; proprietary parts toll every flight hour; the peace dividend
funds the inverse.* Warts: ETF-diluted engine, pre-paid inverse (+75% trailing DAL).
Confirmed by: EU defense budget follow-through + HEI organic growth. Rejected by:
budget reversal (kill-line), or the EUAD proxy drifting from the thesis (K2 latent).

## Findings (hypotheses about sectors, no position)

**H9 — Space:** *Public toll infrastructure in the space economy gets absorbed before
it can be held* (RKLB→IRDM, Amazon→GSAT). Standing prediction: any future public space
tollbooth will attract acquisition within ~24 months. If a standalone toll survives
public for two years, H9 weakens and the sector reopens.

**H10 — AI-datacenter power:** *No tradable instrument feeds on an AI-capex bust; the
inverse seat does not exist publicly.* Standing prediction: if the bust comes, its
profits will accrue to private/distressed vehicles, not listed equities. A new listing
that genuinely monetizes bust economics (distressed DC REIT, power-arbitrage vehicle)
falsifies H10 and reopens the sector.

## Meta-hypotheses (about the method itself)

**M1 — Pre-registration beats conviction:** in 4 of 6 gauntlets, the scout's favorite
seat died on data (farmland REITs, BLDR, drone engines, TDG). Prediction: this rate
persists; scout intuition is a candidate generator, not a picker.

**M2 — Pre-paid inverses underperform their seat:** DVA and DAL entered after +67%/+75%
years. Prediction: their inversion payoff, if the regime turns, will be materially
smaller than seat theory implies. Grade at horizon.

**M3 — Tollbooth survival is regime-dependent, not intrinsic:** TDG's negative year and
the housing flow-toll extinction suggest tolls starve when their *volume* is the regime
casualty. Corollary now embedded in the method: always ask whether the toll is on flow
or on stock.
